I am a fourth year Computer Engineering undergraduate student at The University of Michigan with an interest in Software Development, Mobile Development, Embedded Systems, Software Security, and Business.

Smart Room Project

Member of a team of four that took first place for product design in health and wellness during Makeathon 2016 at The University of Michigan. Team fully implemented a scale model of a smart room by programming an Arduino to control an analog RGB LED strip and a DC motor. Smart room helps the user stabilize his/her Circadian rhythm by affecting melatonin levels throughout the day.
